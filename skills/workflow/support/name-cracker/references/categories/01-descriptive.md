# Descriptive names

Names that directly state what the thing does. The name is the explanation: a reader who has never seen the product understands its function from the name alone.

## Techniques

- `[Function] + [Object]` — the action plus what it acts on (Google Calendar, ImageMagick)
- `[Audience] + [Tool]` — who it is for plus what it is (Visual Studio Code)
- `[Domain] + [Action]` — the field plus what happens in it (Docker Desktop, FileZilla)

## Examples

Google Calendar, Docker Desktop, Visual Studio Code, FileZilla, ImageMagick

## Pitfalls

- Generic by construction — hard to trademark, and free domains are scarce.
- Easy to confuse with every competitor that describes itself the same way.
